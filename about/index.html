StackOverflow Profile:

Experience in Computer Vision

During my first two years working at GRADIANT, I learned about facial feature tracking. We implemented our own version of a Mean-Shift based Constrained Local Model (in the fashion of [Saragih2009]), as well as other types of CLM and AAM -based systems. My master thesis summarises most of this work in face tracking and its application to expression recognition and pose detection.

Later on, I have worked in video and scene analysis projects where I had the opportunity of learning about object detection and tracking. We have explored the possibilities of bayesian filtering for tracking in videosurveillance environments in projects like a2vises and videoguard.

I have also participated in two European Projects (MAVEN and CAESARIS), where we have developed different systems for industrial partners. Some of the implemented modules in these projects included text detection and terrain segmentation using state-of-the-art algorithms.
